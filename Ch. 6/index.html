Planets

Planets are large celestial bodies that orbit a star. Our solar system contains eight planets that revolve around the Sun, each with unique chracteristics and atomspheres.

Stars

Stars are giant balls of hot gas that produce light and heat through nuclear fusion. They come in various sizes and colors, and form constellations across the night sky.

Galaxies

Galaxies are massive systems made up of stars, gas, dust, and dark matter. The Milky Way, where our solar system resides, is just one of billions of galaxies in the universe.
